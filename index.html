About Me

Hello, my name is Elizabeth Saunders, a student currently studying at Confederation College in the IMD (Interactive Media Developement) program, studying towards an Ontario College Advanced Diploma.

I've had a few opportunities to have my artwork up in the Thunder Bay Art Gallery. As well as a couple opportunities to compete in the Skills Ontario Competition for Architectural Technology and Design, and the Graphic Design Studio Production Scopes. A few of my images found in the Photography section have also been published on Pixabay. Also, opportunities to create logo designs for organizations such as, Thunder Bay District 5-Pin Association.

On this portfolio you will find my love of graphic design, photography, and illustration within the many projects done for clients and personal use. Click Below to view in Portfolio.
